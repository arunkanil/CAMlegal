We Are Best For
Law Firm

CAM Legal is a leading full-service law firm in Kochi dedicated to providing pioneering legal solutions to domestic and international clients from diverse sectors.

Established in 2005, CAM Legal is committed and consistent in providing exemplary service to its clients. We are a team of dedicated and experienced litigation lawyers based in Cochin and practising before the High Court of Kerala and its subordinate courts.

Partners of the firm combine years of specialised experience with a litigation practice to offer comprehensive counsel on a range of legal services including banking and finance, construction and engineering, education, energy, hospitality, insurance, automobile, sports, media etc. Our area of practice includes Constitutional law, Civil law, Family law, Administrative law, Service law and Maritime law. Our expertise and proficiency in service litigation at Administrative Tribunals are reputable and long-standing.

CAM Legal is devoted to our client needs by providing legal advice with perception, reliability and responsibility. Our energetic, experienced and enthusiastic team ensures perfection to identify the prospects involved in each case. We value and also seek opinions from our clients to allow us to understand their current and future needs to resolve and mitigate their risks. This makes us one of the most trusted, consistent and reliable litigation teams in Kerala.
