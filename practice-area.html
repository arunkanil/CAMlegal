Constitutional Law

The Constitution of India is the supreme law of the country and it lays down the principles for fundamental political code, structure, procedures, powers, and duties of government institutions and sets out duties, obligations and rights of citizens. In case of any violation of fundamental rights these rights can be enforced through appropriate proceedings. Having dealt with a plethora of cases relating to Constitutional law, CAM Legal has solid experience in handling constitutional matters with expertise. Our services include:

  • (1) Filing and defending of Writs including Habeas Corpus.
  • (2) Grievance evaluation and extent of infringements.
  • (3) Public Interest Litigation (PIL) services.
  • (4) Initiating legal notice demanding redressal for the grievance .
